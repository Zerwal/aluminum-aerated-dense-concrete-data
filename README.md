## Data Description

Six concrete mixtures with aluminum particle 
content ranging from 0% to 5% by mass of cement 
(w/c = 0.35) were tested for:

- Apparent porosity and dry density (ASTM C642)
- Compressive strength at 7, 14, and 28 days 
  (BS EN 12390-3)
- Splitting tensile strength at 28 days 
  (ASTM C496)
- Flexural strength at 28 days (ASTM C78)
